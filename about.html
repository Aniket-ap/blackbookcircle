Our Story

The most valuable conversations happen in private, peer-driven settings where leaders can speak openly, exchange ideas, and collaborate with purpose.

With over a decade of experience working alongside senior executives and global tech leaders, Blackbook Circle was founded to create a new kind of trusted environment, one that brings together enterprise decision-makers and innovators to solve the challenges of today and shape the opportunities of tomorrow.

Our focus is simple: build trust faster, foster meaningful relationships, and deliver measurable results for everyone involved.

Our Mission

To create APAC's most trusted platform for C-suite executives and technology partners to connect, collaborate, and co-create solutions for the future of enterprise.

Our Platform is Built on Three Pillars

C-Suite Leaders

A private, vendor-free peer network for executives to share strategies, validate ideas, and gain clarity through candid conversations under Chatham House Rules.

Tech Vendors

Bespoke GTM and ABM strategies designed to connect you with the right leaders through curated, high-value engagements that accelerate pipeline and build trust.

Advisory & Consulting

Independent consulting services to bridge strategy and execution across CX transformation, digital growth, and leadership innovation.
